== OPTIONS ====================================================================
All options must be provided in the form KEY=VALUE and are separated by &amp;
  
KEY                  VALUE
----------------------------------------
bridgeName           Must be b_Videodisplay
video_url            The URL of the video
autoplay             If playback is started automatically (true/false)
aspectRatio          Explicit specification aspect ratio of the displayed video
defaultZoomButton    Whether there sould be a defaultZoomButton or not (true [default]/false)
videoSizeZoomButton  Whether there sould be a videoSizeZoomButton or not (true [default]/false)
zoomOutButton        Whether there sould be a zoomOutButton or not (true [default]/false)
zoomInButton         Whether there sould be a zoomInButton or not (true [default]/false)
fullScreenButton     Whether there sould be a fullScreenButton or not (true [default]/false)
downloadButton       Whether there sould be a downloadButton or not (true/false [default])
